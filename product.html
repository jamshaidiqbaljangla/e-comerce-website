Returns & Exchanges

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not, we offer a simple return and exchange policy:

  • Returns and exchanges are accepted within 30 days of the delivery date.
  • Items must be in their original condition, unworn/unused, with all original tags and packaging.
  • To initiate a return or exchange, please visit our Returns Portal or contact our customer service team.
  • Once your return is received and inspected, a refund will be processed to your original method of payment within 3-5 business days.
  • Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is defective or incorrect.

For detailed information on our return process, please visit our Returns Policy page.

International Shipping

We ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location:

  • Canada: 5-7 business days, starting at $15.95
  • Europe: 7-10 business days, starting at $24.95
  • Australia/New Zealand: 10-14 business days, starting at $29.95
  • Rest of World: 14-21 business days, starting at $34.95

Please note that international orders may be subject to import duties and taxes, which are the customer's responsibility to pay upon delivery.

Warranty Information

Our Signature Collection items come with a Lifetime Limited Warranty that covers manufacturing defects. This warranty includes:

  • Manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship
  • Structural integrity issues
  • Premature wear under normal use conditions

The warranty does not cover damage resulting from misuse, accidents, improper care, or normal wear and tear.

To submit a warranty claim, please contact our customer service department with your order number and photos of the defect.
